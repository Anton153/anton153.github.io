About

Hi, I'm Anton Shoham — an Engineering Physics student at UBC who enjoys building practical systems across plasma, robotics, controls, and embedded software.

I’ve worked on a hollow cathode development project at Marathon Fusion (plasma centrifuge testbed, DAQ & control electronics), firmware and controls on UBC Orbit’s CubeSat, and previously built a high‑temperature thermal cycler at MKS Instruments. On the fun side, I’ve designed two burger‑building robots and like lightweight simulation + control projects in ROS.
